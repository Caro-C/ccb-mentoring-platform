Our Mission <3
'We aim to empower Women to enter Tech, stay in Tech and succeed in Tech through Mentoring and a supportive female Network.'



With a share of less than 25%, women are still severely underrepresented in the Tech industry. With CoffeeCodeBreak we would like to contribute to more diversity in the Tech sector. Not only for more economic eqality. We firmly believe that our future needs more inclusive digital products. And these are created when women are involved in their development.

We are guided by role models and tend to follow a similar path when we feel empowered to do so. Research has shown that mentoring for women by women has a positive influence on self-confidence, sense of belonging, career planning and professional success. Female role models and mentors in the Tech sector are therefore one of the keys to more diverse teams. They can inspire, motivate to persevere and provide access to valuable resources.

CoffeeCodeBreak connects mentees and mentors in the Tech sector and offers a network of exciting women who support each other.

<3

Who is CoffeeCodeBreak for?
For you as a mentee if you:

Have no to several years of experience in Tech

Are curious about working in Tech and looking for insights and inspiration

Are already working in Tech and would like to talk to more experienced women about everyday work, challenges, development opportunities, etc.

Are actively looking for a mentor and want to build a network
For you as a mentor if you:

Have about 2 or more years of experience in Tech

Would like to share your experience with other women and accompany them in their development

Want to give something back because you know how valuable role models and a good network are for yourself

Would also like to learn from the experience of your mentees and expand your network
How does it work? <3

We connect you as a mentee with a mentor who fits your profile.

From personal experience, it can be incredibly inspiring and helpful to have an intensive chat with a more experienced person even just a few times. We suggest that you meet about 2-3 times within 2 months for a - virtual or real - exchange. (Please consider the current Corona rules.)
Whether a long-term mentoring relationship evolves from this is entirely individual - because in our opinion, sustainable mentoring relationships cannot be forced. Therefore we offer our mentees the opportunity to get to know several mentors. We would even like to encourage you to set up a 'board of mentors', because each mentor has a different focus and experience to share with you. In this way, we not only help to find role models, but also promote the development of a network for both sides.



Since there is no question for us that every woman in the world should have unrestricted access to education, knowledge and support, our offer is of course free of charge.
